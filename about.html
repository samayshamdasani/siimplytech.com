About

siimplytech was founded with one goal: to immerse readers into the vast world of technology. Built and maintained by technology enthusiasts on the cutting edge, we promise to deliver high-quality content in a siimple fashion.

siimplytech.com launched in August 2016. With so many other tech blogs out there, we wanted to be different. That's why we've been focusing entirely on the content that you would actually want to read. Spanning from news, reviews, emerging tech, and how-to's, siimplytech is a site that you'll definitely want to check regularly.
